About

I am a post-doctoral researcher at AMD Research. I completed my Ph.D. studies in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Pennsylvania State University, in 2015, where I worked with Dr. Chita R. Das and Mahmut T. Kandemir in High Performance Computing Lab.

Research Interests

Broadly, my research interests are in the area of computer architecture. Specifically, I work in GPU architectures, and CPU-GPU heterogeneous architectures. I am also interested in NoCs.
